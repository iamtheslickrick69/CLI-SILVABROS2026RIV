- **UL 2703:** Rack mounting safety

## Pre-Hurricane Preparation

### Before Hurricane Season (June 1)

1. **Visual inspection:** Check for loose panels, debris
2. **Tree trimming:** Remove overhanging branches
3. **Documentation:** Photograph system for insurance
4. **Battery charge:** Ensure full capacity
5. **Generator backup:** If you have one, test it

### When Storm Is Approaching

1. **Charge battery:** Maximize stored energy
2. **Reduce loads:** Prioritize essential circuits
3. **Secure loose items:** Around array area
4. **Document system:** Pre-storm photos
5. **Review insurance:** Confirm coverage

### During the Storm

- System automatically manages power
- Battery provides backup if grid fails
- No action needed from you
- Stay safe indoors

### After the Storm

1. **Visual inspection only:** Do not climb on roof
2. **Check monitoring app:** Verify system status
3. **Document any damage:** Photos for insurance
4. **Contact installer:** If issues observed
5. **File claim if needed:** Provide documentation
